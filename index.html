Brace yourself for frequently asked questions because we know you can't get enough of them.

Where Curiosity Meets
Consistency

  • What is ASPHE, and what does it offer to healthcare facilities?

    The Academy of Safe Patient Handling Excellence (ASPHE) is dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare through validated safe patient handling and mobility (SPHM) programs. ASPHE offers a validated SPHM program that has been proven effective in reducing injuries and improving patient outcomes.

  • How does ASPHE ensure the reliability and effectiveness of its programs?

    ASPHE-approved programs undergo a rigorous validation process, ensuring their effectiveness and reliability in real-world healthcare settings. This validation process guarantees that healthcare facilities can trust the program's ability to enhance patient safety and reduce the risk of injuries to both patients and staff.

  • What are the key benefits of implementing an ASPHE-approved program?

    Implementing an ASPHE-approved program offers several benefits, including: Reduction in the risk of injuries to patients and staff. Cost savings from reduced worker's compensation claims. Enhanced patient outcomes and overall quality of care. Alignment with industry standards set by organizations such as the Joint Commission, OSHA, and the American Nurses Office.

  • How does ASPHE's validation system contribute to reducing liability and risk management concerns for healthcare facilities?

    ASPHE's validation system ensures that healthcare facilities have a proactive risk mitigation strategy in place to address potential challenges associated with patient handling and mobility. By implementing a validated program, facilities can reduce liability and risk management concerns, focusing on providing exceptional care while minimizing potential risks.

  • What is the significance of ASPHE's seal of excellence for healthcare facilities?

    ASPHE's seal of excellence signifies that a healthcare facility's SPHM program has been validated and meets the highest standards of safety and effectiveness. It provides recognition for the facility's commitment to excellence in patient care and can help elevate its standards in the healthcare industry.

  • How can healthcare facilities get ASPHE certified?

    Healthcare facilities can contact ASPHE to learn more about how their program can be validated and certified. By partnering with ASPHE, facilities can access a comprehensive and standardized approach to safe patient handling, ensuring the highest level of patient safety and staff well-being.

  • Is ASPHE only for hospitals and skilled nursing facilities, or can other healthcare institutions benefit from its programs?

    While ASPHE initially targets hospitals and skilled nursing facilities, other healthcare institutions such as group homes and long-term care centers can also benefit from its validated SPHM programs. ASPHE aims to promote excellence in safe patient handling practices across various healthcare settings.

  • How does ASPHE ensure ongoing support for healthcare facilities after certification?

    ASPHE offers subscription services to healthcare facilities that want ongoing support in maintaining and updating their safe patient handling programs. These services ensure that facilities remain up to date with the latest best practices and continue to prioritize patient safety and staff well-being.

  • Can ASPHE help healthcare facilities with insurance discounts?

    Yes, healthcare facilities with ASPHE-certified SPHM programs may be eligible for insurance discounts. ASPHE's validation system reduces the risk of injuries, leading to potential cost savings from reduced worker's compensation claims. Facilities can inquire about insurance discounts through ASPHE's accreditation process.

  • How does ASPHE contribute to the overall mission of improving patient safety and healthcare quality

    ASPHE's mission is to promote excellence in safe patient handling practices within healthcare facilities, ultimately improving patient safety and healthcare quality. By validating and recognizing organizations that prioritize patient safety, ASPHE contributes to creating a safer and more efficient healthcare environment for both patients and healthcare providers.
